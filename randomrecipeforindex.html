RECIPE FOR ALOO Paratha

    
    
      
    
        
    
      1. Ingredients
            For the Dough:
                2 cups whole wheat flour
                1/2 teaspoon salt
                Water (as needed)
                1 tablespoon oil (optional)
        2. 
        
        
    
        2. 
        
        • For the Potato Filling:
          • 
        
        •             4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
          • 
        
        •             1 small onion, finely chopped
          • 
        
        •             2 green chilies, finely chopped
          • 
        
        •             1 teaspoon grated ginger
          • 
        
        •             1 teaspoon cumin seeds
          • 
        
        •             1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
          • 
        
        •             1 teaspoon red chili powder
          • 
        
        •             1 teaspoon garam masala
          • 
        
        •             1 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
          • 
        
        •             2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander (cilantro) leaves
          • 
        
        •             Salt to taste
          • 
        
        •             2 tablespoons oil or ghee (clarified butter)
          • 
    
        
        
        3. 
    
      3. For Cooking:
        
                Ghee or oil for cooking the parathas
        4. 
        

      5. Instructions
            Preparing the Dough:
        
                
      6. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it is smooth and soft.
        7. 
                
      7. Optionally, add 1 tablespoon of oil to make the dough more pliable.
        8. 
                
      8. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 20-30 minutes.
        9. 
    
        Preparing the Potato Filling:
        
        
      9. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat.
        10. 
        
      10. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
        11. 
        
      11. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
        12. 
        
      12. Add grated ginger and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
        13. 
        
      13. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
        14. 
        
      14. Add the boiled and mashed potatoes to the pan. Mix thoroughly.
        15. 
        
      15. Add amchur powder and chopped fresh coriander leaves. Mix well.
        16. 
        
      16. Cook the mixture for a few minutes until everything is well combined.
        17. 
        
      17. Remove from heat and let it cool.
        18. 
        
        Assembling and Cooking Aloo Parathas:
        
            Divide the dough into equal-sized balls.
            Take one dough ball and roll it into a small disc using a rolling pin.
            Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of the disc.
            Bring the edges of the dough together to cover the filling, forming a ball again.
            Gently flatten the stuffed dough ball with your hands.
            Roll it out carefully into a paratha, being cautious not to let the filling spill out.
            Heat a tawa or skillet over medium-high heat.
            Place the rolled-out paratha on the hot tawa.
            Cook until small bubbles start forming on the surface, then flip it over.
            Apply ghee or oil on both sides and cook until golden brown spots appear and the paratha is cooked through.
            Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.
